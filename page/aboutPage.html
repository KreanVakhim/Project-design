Our History

Coffee Cambodia was founded in 2018 with a mission to bring premium coffee to Phnom Penh. Over the years, we have grown into a community of coffee lovers who appreciate quality and sustainability.

From our humble beginnings as a small coffee cart, we've expanded to multiple locations across the city, each with its own unique charm and atmosphere. Our commitment to quality never wavered.

Our Coffee Varieties

1. Espresso

Strong, rich, concentrated coffee.

2. Americano

Espresso diluted with hot water.

3. Latte

Espresso with steamed milk.

4. Cappuccino

Espresso with frothed milk and cocoa powder.

5. Mocha

Chocolate-flavored coffee with espresso and milk.

6. Macchiato

Espresso marked with milk foam.

7. Flat White

Similar to latte but with velvety microfoam milk.

8. Affogato

Espresso poured over vanilla ice cream.

9. Irish Coffee

Hot coffee with whiskey, sugar, and cream.

10. Iced Coffee

Cold coffee served with ice and milk/syrup.

11. Turkish Coffee

Finely ground coffee simmered in water and sugar.

12. Cold Brew

Slow brewed coffee in cold water for smooth flavor.

Our Coffee Sources

We source our coffee beans from premium plantations across Cambodia and neighboring countries. Each batch is carefully selected to ensure freshness, aroma, and sustainable farming practices.

Our direct relationships with farmers allow us to support local communities and maintain the highest quality standards from bean to cup.
